About
What is DeepFake Detector?

DeepFake Detector is a free AI-powered tool that helps you verify the authenticity of any video. With the rise of AI-generated content, it has become harder to tell what is real. Our tool uses advanced machine learning to analyze facial patterns, pixel inconsistencies, and audio-visual mismatches to give you an accurate result in seconds.

Why Use It
Why Does This Matter?

Deepfakes are being used to spread misinformation, fraud, and fake news. Here is why you should verify videos before trusting them.

📰

Stop Misinformation

Fake videos of politicians, celebrities, and public figures are spreading rapidly on social media. Verify before you share.

🛡️

Protect Yourself

Deepfakes are used in scams and fraud. Detecting them early can protect you from being deceived or manipulated.

⚖️

Legal Evidence

In legal or professional settings, confirming a video is authentic adds credibility and trust to evidence.

🧠

Stay Informed

As AI gets better, so do fakes. Using a detector keeps you one step ahead of manipulated content.
